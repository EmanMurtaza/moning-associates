About Steven

More Than a
Realtor — A
Trusted Advisor

Steven Moning has been a dedicated real estate professional since 2006, bringing nearly two decades of hands-on market experience to every client engagement. Holding a Master's degree in Information Systems, he applies a data-driven, analytical mindset to real estate — an edge that consistently delivers superior results.

Based in the Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex, Steven specializes in REO properties, BPO valuations, investor acquisitions, luxury homes, and residential sales. His reputation is built not just on transactions closed, but on relationships built for life.

As CEO of Moning & Associates and an eXp Realty agent, Steven leads a talented team united by one goal: making your real estate experience exceptional.
